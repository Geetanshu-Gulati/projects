Raspberry Lemon Drop Martini

Ingredients

  • 10 fresh raspberries, or more to taste
  • 2 fluid ounces lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 fluid ounces vodka
  • 1 cup ice as needed
  • 1 lemon wedge

Recipie

  1. Muddle 8 raspberries in the bottom of a cocktail shaker with lemon juice and sugar. Add vodka and ice. Shake for 1 minute, then strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with remaining raspberries and a lemon wedge.
